About Us

Blacksmiths is a clothing retailer headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, We are currently one of the West Africa's largest garment importers of formal/casual menswear and ladieswear.

Blacksmiths is a company devoted to selling men and women’s clothings with a great look and with high technical quality as well as to supply services of the highest level It specialises in the selling of clothing for confident men and women who know their own style.

We have grown rapidly and the brand has become synonymous with the highest levels of quality at a fantastic price. We have been fortunate enough to accumulate a large number of loyal customers who recognize the special blend of quality and value

The products we supply are sourced from key manufacturing partners in in Europe, and Asia. Our manufacturing partner’s values craftsmanship; an attention to detail so finely tuned that style, comfort and functionality flow seamlessly We have positioned ourselves to serve our customers successfully via our catalogues, online shop We attach, more than ever, great importance to personal advice and first-class service via all sales channels.

Our goal is to exceed the expectations of our customers on a regular basis and to inspire them with our high-quality fashion and our service. We want to give our customers the feel-good factor of alway being dressed appropriately in our fashions. We sell clothing from manufacturers that utilize the world’s finest fabrics with personalized construction to create classic clothes. And we provide an incredible value and offer unparalleled customer service.

We focuses on clothings with meticulous precision and uses only the finest of fabrics that blend together traditional and contemporary styles

Blacksmiths.com.ng publishes Blacksmith Fashion an online magazine, The Journal, put together by a team of dedicated editors , provides unrivalled style advice and customer care.

Our Mission Statement

" We must have a passion for everything we do: for fashion and design: for creating long term professional partnerships: for operating ethically. Our goal is to become the number one supplier of men's and women's clothing to the world's best retailers."
